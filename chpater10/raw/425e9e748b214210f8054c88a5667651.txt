<div class="longText">
<p>The US-based Atlantic Council has been listed as a "security threat" in Russia and is set to be banned, Russian prosecutors have said.</p> <p><a href="/en/huntsman-takes-up-moscow-post-at-a-time-of-historically-poor-relations/a-40790966">US ambassador in Moscow, Jon Huntsman</a>, chaired the pro-Western NGO between 2014 and 2017.</p> <p>On Thursday, the prosecutors said the Atlantic Council's activities "pose a threat to fundamentals of constitutional order and security of the Russian federation."</p> <p>They did not provide details on the move.</p> <p><strong>'We are disappointed'</strong></p> <p>Once the decision is processed by the nation's justice ministry, the think tank is set to join the likes of <a href="/en/soros-funded-foundation-to-close-its-doors-in-turkey/a-46461472">George Soros' Open Society Foundation</a> on the banned international NGO list, which currently has 17 names. These organizations are prohibited from operating on Russian territory and Russian citizens are not allowed to work for them, according to Russian news outlet Meduza.</p> <p>The Atlantic Council chairman Frederick Kempe said they were aware of the reports on their ban, but had received no notice from the Russian state.</p> <p>"Russian authorities have not informed us, either formally or informally, about this decision," Kempe said in a statement carried by Russian news agency Interfax on Friday.</p> <p>"We are disappointed by this decision and are studying its consequences," Kempe said.</p> <div class="col3 right"> <div class="group"> <div class="standaloneWrap"> <div class="imgTeaserL video" data-media-id="45072569"> <div class="mediaItem" data-media-id="45072569"> <input type="hidden" name="player_type" value="video" /> <input type="hidden" name="file_name" value="https://tvdownloaddw-a.akamaihd.net/dwtv_video/flv/je/je20180814_ngo08b_sd_vp6.flv" /> <input type="hidden" name="file_duration" value="188" /> <input type="hidden" name="display_date" value="20180814" /> <input type="hidden" name="media_id" value="45072569" /> <input type="hidden" name="media_title" value="Russian government withholds funding for prominent NGO" /> <input type="hidden" name="dk_content" value="false" /> <input type="hidden" name="preview_image" value="https://tvdownloaddw-a.akamaihd.net/stills/images/je/je20180814_ngo08b_image_1024x576_3.jpg" /> <div class="teaserImg" title="Russian NGO fights for survival" alt="Russian NGO fights for survival"> <div class="videoContainer" id="videoContainer-45072569" rel="dynamic videoData for javascript"></div> <div class="playButtonArea"> <div class="customPlayBtn"> <div class="playBtnImgBox"></div> <span class="playBtnText">Watch video</span> <span class="right">03:08</span> </div> </div> </div> <script type="text/javascript">
