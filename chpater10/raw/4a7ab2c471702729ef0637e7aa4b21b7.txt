<section class="ea-post-title">

    <h1>Dozens of migrants still stuck on coastguard vessel in Italy port</h1></section>

<section class="row">
    <article class="ea-article col-xs-12 col-sm-12 col-md-8 col-lg-8">
        <div class="ea-article-header">
            <div class="ea-article-meta clearfix">
                <p class="ea-byline"> <a href='https://www.euractiv.com/content_providers/euractiv-com-with-afp/'>EURACTIV.com with AFP</a> </p><p><span class="ea-dateformat" data-timestamp="1564383433">
            <i class="fa fa-calendar"></i> 29-07-2019
        </span></p>            </div>
            <div class="ea-article-header-content">
                <div class="ea-article-featured-image">
        <span class="ea-image-aspect-16x9"><img width="800" height="450" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" data-lazy-type="image" data-lazy-src="https://www.euractiv.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2019/07/Gregoretti-800x450.jpg" class="lazy lazy-hidden attachment-16x9 size-16x9 wp-post-image" alt="" /><noscript><img width="800" height="450" src="https://www.euractiv.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2019/07/Gregoretti-800x450.jpg" class="attachment-16x9 size-16x9 wp-post-image" alt="" /></noscript></span>
    </div><div class="ea-image-meta clearfix"><p>The Gregoretti coastguard ship [@Francescorocca Twitter]</p></div>            </div>
        </div>

        <div class="ea-article-body">
            <div class="ea-article-meta clearfix">
                <p class="ea-social vsac-social"><a href="#ea-comments" target="_self" class="vsac-social-comments btn btn-sm"><i class="fa fa-commenting"></i><span class="hidden-xs hidden-sm">Comments</span></a> <a href="javascript:window.print()" target="_self" class="vsac-social-print btn btn-sm"><i class="fa fa-print"></i><span class="hidden-xs hidden-sm">Print</span></a> <a href="//www.reddit.com/submit?url=https://www.euractiv.com/section/justice-home-affairs/news/dozens-of-migrants-still-stuck-on-coastguard-vessel-in-italy-port/&title=Dozens of migrants still stuck on coastguard vessel in Italy port" target="_blank" class="vsac-social-reddit btn btn-sm"><i class="fa fa-reddit"></i><span class="hidden-xs hidden-sm"> </span></a> </p>            </div>
            <div class="ea-article-body-content">
                <p>An Italian coastguard vessel stranded in the Mediterranean with more than 130 migrants aboard has been allowed to dock in the Sicilian port of Augusta but Rome on Sunday (28 July) refused to let them disembark until a deal is struck with the EU.</p>
<p>&#8220;The Gregoretti berthed in the port of Augusta overnight, as is the normal procedure for a military vessel. Now the EU has to act because the migration question concerns the whole continent,&#8221; Transport Minister Danilo Toninelli said in a statement.</p>
<p>Some 140 migrants, who had set off from Libya in two rickety boats, were picked up by Italian patrols on Thursday night and transferred to the coastguard ship Bruno Gregoretti.</p>
<div class="clearfix">
<blockquote class="twitter-tweet" data-width="500" data-dnt="true">
<p lang="it" dir="ltr">Stallo sulla Gregoretti. Salvini resta solo, nessuna sponda da Conte e Mattarella <a href="https://t.co/c4rr6E54DH">https://t.co/c4rr6E54DH</a> <a href="https://t.co/dyWUs6IQLf">pic.twitter.com/dyWUs6IQLf</a></p>
<p>&mdash; La Stampa (@LaStampa) <a href="https://twitter.com/LaStampa/status/1155706691393953793?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw">July 29, 2019</a></p></blockquote>
<p><script async src="https://platform.twitter.com/widgets.js" charset="utf-8"></script></div>
<p>The operation took place on the same day that at least 115 other migrants were feared drowned in a shipwreck off Libya &#8212; the Mediterranean&#8217;s deadliest tragedy this year, according to the International Organization for Migration (IOM).</p>
<p>Several migrants aboard the Gregoretti have already been evacuated for medical attention, including a seven-month pregnant woman, her two children and her partner.</p>
<p>The ship&#8217;s crew is blocked on board along with the migrants.</p>
<p>However, Italy&#8217;s far-right Interior Minister Matteo Salvini has reiterated that the remaining migrants would not be able to leave the vessel until other European countries agree to take them in.</p>
<p>Salvini, also deputy prime minister, has taken a hard line against migrants rescued at sea being brought to Italy, which he says bears an unfair burden in the crisis.</p>
<p>French President Emmanuel Macron announced last Monday that 14 EU members had approved a plan to redistribute refugees rescued in the Mediterranean, and eight said they would actively take part.</p>
<p>The proposal drew Salvini&#8217;s ire because it still involved allowing migrants to disembark on his country&#8217;s territory.</p>
<p>He said the agreement underscored a demand that Italy &#8220;continue to be the refugee camp of Europe&#8221;.</p>
<p>&#8211; &#8216;Act swiftly&#8217; &#8211;</p>
<p>Pope Francis on Sunday called on the international community to &#8220;act swiftly&#8221; to help avoid further deaths.</p>
<p>&#8220;I have learned with sorrow the news of the dramatic shipwreck that happened in recent days in the Mediterranean where dozens of migrants including women and children have lost their lives,&#8221; he said Sunday during his weekly Angelus address on St Peter&#8217;s Square.</p>
<p>&#8220;I am renewing my call that the international community act swiftly and decisively to avoid that such tragedies repeat themselves and guarantee the safety and dignity of all.&#8221;</p>
<p>Former Italian navy chief Giuseppe De Giorgi, who launched the Mare Nostrum maritime rescue plan in 2013, hailed the Gregoretti&#8217;s crew who &#8220;despite all were committed to accomplishing with honour their duty as sailors to protect lives at sea.&#8221;</p>
<p>In August 2018, almost more than 150 people were stranded on the Italian coastguard ship Diciotti for over a week before an agreement between the Italian church, Albania and Ireland allowed them to disembark.</p>
