<h1 class='post-title'>
German government very concerned about arrests in Russia 
</h1>
<div class='post-meta'>
<span class='post-author'><a href='https://www.blogger.com/profile/13840391367277191627' target='_blank' title='Cik Bunga'>Cik Bunga</a></span>

BERLIN (Reuters) - The German government 
said it was very concerned about Russian police arresting more than 
1,000 people in Moscow on Saturday around a protest to demand that 
opposition members be allowed to run in a local election.<br />

opposition decrying President Vladimir Putin's tight grip on power.<br />
