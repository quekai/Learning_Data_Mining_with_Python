The bill was eventually suspended, but protesters demands have grown to include direct elections, the dissolution of the current legislature and an investigation into alleged police brutality.</p>		<p>

The arrangement promises the city certain democratic freedoms that are not afforded mainland citizens, but some residents say these liberties are now under threat.</p><p>A distrust of Chinas Communist Party-led central government in Beijing has undergirded the protests.
