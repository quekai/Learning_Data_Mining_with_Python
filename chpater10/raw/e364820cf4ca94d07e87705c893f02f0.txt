                            <div class="c-formatted c-post-content">
                                <p><span id="urn:enhancement-8f9a2b2c-ffcf-a514-4c91-d579c26f2301" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/venezuela"><span id="urn:local-text-annotation-hpbym5ege93o67s0j6qf16mszq46o52n" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/venezuela">Venezuela</span></span> appears to be leveraging <span id="urn:enhancement-3cf4d934-c378-abc3-f4a1-b9c632a4174a" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/cryptocurrency"><span id="urn:local-text-annotation-s63jqg231wr2lmsnogoehrxwdswi4cy1" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/cryptocurrency">cryptocurrencies</span></span> as part of a larger effort to bypass <span id="urn:enhancement-f58aacb5-e51f-cba5-2d3b-19d44aacdbdc">US</span> <span id="urn:enhancement-7f9fe618-dd3a-f383-2983-5a317715ac93"><span id="urn:local-text-annotation-mgr7a0vsxzhgsqrsh61gxe88jnup1ibx" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/international_sanctions">sanctions</span></span>.</p>
<p>According to <a href="https://www.abc.es/internacional/abci-maduro-tasas-aeroportuarias-para-burlar-sanciones-eeuu-201907212232_noticia.html" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">an investigation by Spanish newspaper ABC</a>, President Nicolas Maduro and his administration are using a digital wallet app called Jetman Pay to convert <span id="urn:enhancement-2793487d-b2d1-67e7-8af1-e416326c8a14"><span id="urn:local-text-annotation-procuymmrzo6j9h6l7menghdj6c3ia01" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/tax_revenue">tax revenue</span></span> from one of the country’s main airports into <span id="urn:enhancement-ab4abfa9-e953-1ec4-2dca-1f4f4c46471f"><span id="urn:local-text-annotation-kgo761x8ayk4fw6posn252mijktwwf4a" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/bitcoin">Bitcoin</span></span> and other <span id="urn:enhancement-09d79aa5-eca9-8283-c0dc-52dc7f041f9a" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/cryptocurrency"><span id="urn:local-text-annotation-ayk64nq84zl4j06jus5t228ao68dywg9" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/cryptocurrency">cryptocurrencies</span></span>.</p>
            <div class="jwplayer-placeholder">
                <div id="jwplayerInlineAd"></div>
            </div>
            <script src="//cdn.jwplayer.com/libraries/vRZNU7bT.js"> </script>
            <script>
                window.jwLibrary = "AXGgFnXj";

<p>The newspaper claims to have unearthed a scheme whereby <span id="urn:enhancement-a66c76e6-fe2e-ff1f-45ed-976731a02cc5" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/cryptocurrency"><span id="urn:local-text-annotation-b6drry78pcs5jpj5jodmh2npde1zy6q3" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/cryptocurrency">cryptocurrencies</span></span> are sent to foreign <span id="urn:enhancement-4c7090b4-47c8-18d7-2a0c-72937a2b23c3"><span id="urn:enhancement-1b45b18c-f227-fc20-72ea-0e9bab5fe947">exchanges</span></span> in <span id="urn:enhancement-855d49a8-700c-b0ae-94ae-01732f666e1d"><span id="urn:enhancement-d530e43b-54de-423d-2fc1-a630992dcc01">Russia</span></span>, <span id="urn:enhancement-0656f468-6e34-5da3-b050-1bfdf3949bef"><span id="urn:enhancement-c9109511-9719-eea0-bfdf-d8e3512309cd">China</span></span>, <span id="urn:enhancement-ebcde3d6-5a52-9287-0b19-0e545486e795"><span id="urn:enhancement-434293b9-7590-257b-2d2b-5866f6ac51d5">Hong Kong</span></span>, and <span id="urn:enhancement-581c961b-402c-eaf3-4c0a-c31635867feb"><span id="urn:enhancement-b34078e0-49c3-365f-171c-10917f9a94d2">Hungary</span></span>. Once received, the funds are converted into <span id="urn:enhancement-d7acab92-920b-feb4-9c88-9b8c465d0f35"><span id="urn:local-text-annotation-e727anxc79irujgkv657sn18bfaifcwi" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/united_states_dollar">US</span> dollars</span> and sent back to <span id="urn:enhancement-ae8cedd8-2ac6-62f3-274d-05217f480bc8" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/venezuela"><span id="urn:local-text-annotation-ueof4l4fc3rk6yasqtxtuc2krig86pac" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/venezuela">Venezuela</span></span>.</p>
<p>For the moment, Jetman Pay is only being used by Maiquetia International Airport (IAIM), but <span id="urn:enhancement-ad1b9828-b3b4-0c59-62b5-d0668cde8b04"><span id="urn:local-text-annotation-il54xaxujfraplhep6belj8p3qv5a1nt" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/nicolas_maduro">Maduro</span></span> and his <span id="urn:enhancement-61607888-b2b3-5236-061c-ad795b279d4b"><span id="urn:local-text-annotation-491m73um16wm66modjidp5xholjzhv2c" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/government">government</span></span> are thought to be holding talks to expand the <span id="urn:enhancement-d1a42450-f598-b4bf-6879-54382af39b10">app</span>‘s use.</p>
<p><span id="urn:enhancement-27fb8141-6911-2d80-0965-b395f851e568">Venezuela</span>‘s economy has been <a href="https://www.nytimes.com/2017/01/16/world/americas/nuevos-billetes-venezuela-new-banknotes.html" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">battling severe hyperinflation</a> for years and cryptocurrency seems to have piqued <span id="urn:local-text-annotation-ga0u5rl83jgr51zibiwco6hr2ek5dzrs" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/nicolas_maduro">Maduro</span>‘s interest in recent times.</p>
<p>Earlier this month, <a href="https://thenextweb.com/hardfork/2019/07/05/maduro-orders-venezuelan-bank-to-sell-petro-cryptocurrency-in-latest-pump-attempt/">he ordered</a> that every branch of the Banco de <span id="urn:enhancement-f3e68f4e-1964-45db-8e2d-34c5b4b90da8">Venezuela</span> should let <span id="urn:enhancement-ab0850cf-190e-8030-f521-a5ebd4a866f6">citizens</span> buy and trade El Petro, the state-issued <span id="urn:enhancement-813f2e8f-652f-aeee-6e01-723772780fe6">cryptocurrency</span> he’s hoping will provide a much-needed workaround economic <span id="urn:local-text-annotation-yquvfmpoo49a73e3t8fuguzy8uop85ez" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/international_sanctions">sanctions</span>.</p>
<p>El Petro was first introduced last year, after <span id="urn:local-text-annotation-rpy70qux0sm1osabt62bop16im424njb" itemid="http://data.thenextweb.com/tnw/entity/nicolas_maduro">Maduro</span> said on a state-run television channel that it would be used in international <span id="urn:enhancement-5d24521c-cc8f-21e4-e628-862b4ae9edfe">transactions</span> starting from October 1, but the <span id="urn:enhancement-c38e0d52-4f17-8701-90d3-3e4a5d60422b">cryptocurrency</span> <a href="https://www.reuters.com/article/us-cryptocurrency-venezuela-specialrepor/special-report-in-venezuela-new-cryptocurrency-is-nowhere-to-be-found-idUSKCN1LF15U" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">failed to catch on</a>.</p>
<p>Although <span id="urn:enhancement-012379c4-818f-4693-8269-d3d245b3cfe4">cryptocurrency</span> is seemingly providing a short-term solution, <span id="urn:enhancement-932a2c5f-7365-85d0-7199-7560ecf31a01">Venezuela</span>‘s problems go well beyond what <span id="urn:enhancement-5c3a2f3f-1e8c-d956-1fcb-ea667e5bc395">Bitcoin</span> et al. could possibly solve.</p>
