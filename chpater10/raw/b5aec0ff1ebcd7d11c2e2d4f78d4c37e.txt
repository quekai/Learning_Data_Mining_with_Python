<div class="topListDate">22 July 2019</div>

<p>Forest fires in Krasnoyarsk region caused the smell of burning and haze.&nbsp;Picture: Asya Vostrikova</p>
</div>
<p>Pictures show the scale of the smoke in major population centres from the raging wildfires with the cause seen mainly as out-of-control forest fires in Krasnoyarsk region, the second largest in Russia.</p>
<p>In Krasnoyarsk city, residents have been told by health officials to go outside only if really necessary.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Here watchdog Rospotrebnadzor confirmed the hygienic standard&nbsp; in the air of the PM 2.5 particles is exceeded by between 1.2&nbsp; and 2.3 times.</p>

<p>Dust, soot and other small particles are shown as present in the air.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Later the sensors showed readings from 221 to 344.&nbsp;</p>
